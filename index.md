## About me

---

Hi! I'm Yingtie Lei. I'm currently an M.S. student in Computer Science and Engineering at **The Ohio State University**, where I work with Prof. [Wei-Lun (Harry) Chao](https://sites.google.com/view/wei-lun-harry-chao/home) and Prof. [Mi Zhang](https://mi-zhang.github.io/). I'm also a remote intern at the **University of Macau**, where I collaborate with Prof. [Chi-Man Pun](https://cmpun.github.io/) and Dr. [Xuhang Chen](https://cxh.netlify.app/). Before OSU, I received my B.E. in Intelligent Science and Technology from **South China University of Technology**.

My research interests include low-level vision and explainability in computer vision, with a broader focus on building more transparent and reliable visual learning systems. Recently, I have also been exploring how agentic systems can be integrated with a range of deep learning tasks.
